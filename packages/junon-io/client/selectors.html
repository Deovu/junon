Selectors

  • @a - all players
  • @m - all mobs
  • @b - all buildings
  • @b[type=MiningDrill] - all buildings that are mining drills
  • @r - random player

Examples

  • /give @a MiniTurret - give everyone miniturret
  • /tp @a 20 20 - teleport everyone to coord 20,20
  • /clear @b[type=MiningDrill] - empty all inventory of mining drills
  • /caption @a[health<20] title danger - send caption to players who have less than 20 health
  • /kill @a[gold>=1000] - kill all players who have gold value of 1000 or more
  • /tp @m[region=jail] 20 20 - teleport all mobs in jail region to coordinate 20 20

Selector Arguments

Selector arguments reduce the number of players/mobs/buildings being selected. @a[role=police] will only select players who have a police role.

  • @a arguments - region, role, team, health, stamina, oxygen, hunger, speed, gold, score
  • @b arguments - region, type, health, owner, team
  • @m arguments - region, type, health, owner, team, hunger, speed
