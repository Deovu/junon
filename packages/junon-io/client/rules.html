Junon.io - Rules

Breaking rules below might result in a ban. Please read carefully. If you see anyone breaking rule, visit our discord channel at https://discord.com/invite/T6rPXtw and report the offending user to one of our moderators. Include screenshots if appropriate.

Respect players

  • No malicious griefing (destroying/spamming buildings without owners permission)
  • No racist, homophopic, hateful speech or excessive swearing. Any harassments, threats, and bullying will not be tolerated.
  • No NSFW (or sexually suggestive or implied) or explicit content
  • No teaming in minigames that disallow teaming
  • No Talking about self-harm, or other deeply negative experiences
  • No Encouraging violence or hatred towards other players
  • No Threating to hack other players
  • No copying other player made games block by block and claiming it as your own.

Cheating and Exploits

  • No hacking allowed. Such as using unexposed api from the server.
  • No exploiting the game to gain unfair advantage. This could include item duplications, infinite gold, or spawning items or mob freely unless the gamemode allows it.
  • Report exploitable bugs to our discord channel to one of our moderators. And if its significant such gold glitch or item duplication, you'll be credited with finding the bug in changelog.
